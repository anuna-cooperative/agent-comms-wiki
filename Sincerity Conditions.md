# Sincerity Conditions

In Searle and Vanderveken's illocutionary logic, the psychological state a speaker must be in for an illocutionary act to be non-defective (belief for assertions, intention for promises, desire for requests). The foundation of the "sincerity" preconditions in mentalistic ACL semantics and the source of the verifiability problem.
