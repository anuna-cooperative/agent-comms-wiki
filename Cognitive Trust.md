# Cognitive Trust

A family of trust models (e.g., Castelfranchi & Falcone) that represent trust as a mental attitude grounded in explicit beliefs about the trustee's competence, willingness, and dependence, rather than as a numeric reputation score. Contrasts with game-theoretic and statistical reputation models in the Sabater–Sierra taxonomy.
