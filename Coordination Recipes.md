# Coordination Recipes

Standard distributed-coordination primitives — shared counters, queues, barriers, leader election — used as benchmarks for coordination kernels. Extensible kernels (EZK, EDS) implement each recipe as a server-side extension, yielding order-of-magnitude throughput gains over client-side composition of ZooKeeper/DepSpace operations.
