## Superpeer Overlay

A two-tier peer-to-peer topology in which a small subset of high-capacity nodes (superpeers) carry routing and indexing load for a larger set of ordinary peers attached to them. Myconet constructs such an overlay via self-organising, fungi-inspired growth rules.
