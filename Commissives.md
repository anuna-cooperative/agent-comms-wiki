# Commissives

In Searle's taxonomy of illocutionary acts, speech acts that commit the speaker to a future course of action (promises, vows, offers). FIPA-ACL was criticised by Chaib-draa and Dignum for lacking native commissive performatives, forcing commitment semantics to be simulated via requests and agree/refuse pairs.
