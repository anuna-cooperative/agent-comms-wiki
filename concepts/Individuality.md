# Individuality

In Levin's computational-boundary-of-a-self framework, the degree to which a system pursues goals at a given level of organisation, defining where one cognitive agent ends and another begins. Operationalised through the cognitive light cone — the spatiotemporal extent of goals a system can represent and act on.
