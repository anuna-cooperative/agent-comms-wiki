# Hyphal Peer

In the Myconet fungal overlay, an intermediate peer state between mobile "biomass" and settled "immobile superpeer": hyphae extend and branch to explore the network, driven by local biomass comparisons via Newscast gossip, eventually solidifying into superpeers when saturated.
