# Merge Operator

The dual device to [[Selection (Choreography)|selection]] in [[Endpoint Projection]]: a partial *merging* operation on local types/behaviours that decides when a receiver's alternative continuations are reconcilable into one local program — i.e. when a role does **not** need to be explicitly told which branch a choice took because its behaviour is the same (or compatibly extendable) across branches. Merging is what lets projection succeed without a [[Selection (Choreography)|selection]] for every conditional; where merging fails, [[Knowledge of Choice|knowledge of choice]] must be restored by [[Choreography Amendment|amendment]].
