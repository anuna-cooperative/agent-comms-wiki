# Social Chatbots

Conversational systems optimised for long-term emotional engagement rather than single-turn task completion, characterised by the integration of IQ (knowledge, reasoning) with EQ (empathy, persona, social skill) and evaluated by sustained-engagement metrics like Conversation-turns Per Session (CPS). Exemplified by Microsoft XiaoIce.
