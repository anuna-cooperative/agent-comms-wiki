# Pushdown Automata

A finite-state automaton augmented with an unbounded stack. Pushdown automata recognize exactly the context-free languages, sitting one level above finite-state machines in the [[Chomsky Hierarchy]], and are the minimal machine strong enough to accept languages like `aⁿbⁿ` that no regular expression can match. In [[LangSec]] they are the canonical recognizer invoked when a validator must be at least as strong as a [[Context-Free Grammars|context-free grammar]] to fully check an input.
