## Turing Test

Turing's imitation game, in which a human judge conducts a text-only conversation with a machine and another human and must decide which is which; sustained indistinguishability is taken as evidence of machine intelligence. Social-chatbot work criticises it as a narrow, adversarial goal and proposes engagement-based alternatives.
