# ZFC

Zermelo–Fraenkel set theory with the Axiom of Choice — the de facto standard axiomatic foundation of mathematics. Begins with [[Untersuchungen über die Grundlagen der Mengenlehre I|Zermelo's 1908 axioms]] (Extensionality, Separation, Power set, Union, Choice, Infinity, Elementary sets), strengthened by Fraenkel and Skolem with the Axiom of Replacement (and Foundation). [[Russell's Paradox]] is blocked by Separation. Famously incomplete in a strong sense: the [[Continuum Hypothesis]] and the [[Axiom of Choice]] are independent of it (Gödel + [[Forcing|Cohen]]).
