## Recipe (SharedPlans)

In Grosz & Kraus's [[SharedPlans]] framework, a *recipe* is a parameterised plan for performing an action: a sequence of sub-actions plus the constraints among them. Multiple recipes may exist for the same action; agents must agree on which to follow as part of forming the SharedPlan. The recipe abstraction separates *what* the action is from *how* it is to be performed, supporting reuse of sub-plans and incremental refinement under partial information.
