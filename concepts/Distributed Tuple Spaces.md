## Distributed Tuple Spaces

A network of [[Tuple Spaces|tuple spaces]] partitioned across multiple physical sites, each typically associated with a [[Locality (KLAIM)|locality]] in the [[KLAIM]] sense. Combines Linda-style content-addressed coordination with location-aware access control and migration semantics. Production realisations include JavaSpaces over Jini, TSpaces, and GigaSpaces; KLAIM is the canonical formal model.
