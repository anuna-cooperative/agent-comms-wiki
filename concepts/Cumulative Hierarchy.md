# Cumulative Hierarchy

The picture of the set-theoretic universe *V* as built up in stages indexed by the [[Ordinals]]: V₀ = ∅, V_{α+1} = 𝒫(V_α), and unions at limit stages, with *V* = ⋃_α V_α. Under the axiom of foundation every set appears at some stage, giving a well-founded, layered universe. The hierarchy is the standard model-theoretic backdrop for [[ZFC]] and for inner models like the [[Constructible Universe]].
