# Producer-Consumer Asymmetry

The structural insight behind Proof-Carrying Code: *producing* a proof of a safety property can be arbitrarily hard, but *checking* one is cheap and mechanical. The burden is therefore shifted onto the untrusted code producer, who must ship a proof, while the consumer runs only a small, trusted [[Logical Framework|proof checker]]. Safety is established with no trust in the producer and no runtime monitoring.
