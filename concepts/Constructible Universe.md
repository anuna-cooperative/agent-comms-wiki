# Constructible Universe

Gödel's inner model *L*, built like the [[Cumulative Hierarchy]] but admitting at each stage only the sets *definable* from earlier ones. *L* is a model of [[ZFC]] in which both the [[Axiom of Choice]] and the [[Continuum Hypothesis]] hold, which is how Gödel (1938–40) proved AC and CH *consistent* with ZF. It is the complement to [[Forcing|Cohen's]] construction of models where they fail — together establishing independence.
