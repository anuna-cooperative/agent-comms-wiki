# Empathetic Response Generation

The design goal, central to social chatbots like XiaoIce, of producing replies that recognise and appropriately address the user's emotional state rather than merely completing a task. Combines affect detection, persona-consistent generation, and long-horizon engagement modelling (CPS as success metric).
