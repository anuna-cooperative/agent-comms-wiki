# Eliza

Weizenbaum's 1966 pattern-matching chatbot simulating a Rogerian psychotherapist using keyword-driven reflection templates. The prototype case for surface-only conversational agents and the origin of the "Eliza effect" — human overattribution of understanding to simple systems.
