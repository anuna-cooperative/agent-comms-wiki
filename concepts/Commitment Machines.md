# Commitment Machines

Yolum & Singh's (ATAL-01) compilation target for commitment-based protocols: the protocol's commitment-state space, with transitions induced by the operations on commitments, can be projected to a finite-state machine that captures all and only the legal protocol runs. Commitment machines combine the executability advantages of FSMs with the meaning-preserving semantics of commitment-based specification — agents that cannot reason logically can still follow the compiled FSM.
