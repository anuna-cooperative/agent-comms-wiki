## Inquiry Dialogue

A [[Dialogue Typology|Walton-Krabbe dialogue type]] whose shared goal is *to establish proof of a hypothesis from agreed premises*. All participants begin in a state of relative ignorance (the question is open) and seek a definite settlement supported by mutually-acceptable evidence. Distinct from [[Persuasion Dialogue|persuasion]] (where one side advances a position before the dialogue) and from [[Information-Seeking Dialogue|information-seeking]] (where one party already knows). Scientific peer review is a paradigm case.
