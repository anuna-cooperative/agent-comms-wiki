## ASN.1 BER Ambiguity

The property of ASN.1 Basic Encoding Rules whereby the same abstract value admits multiple valid byte-level encodings. The resulting non-canonical form enables parser-differential attacks, since different implementations may disagree on which bytes to accept or how to interpret them.
