# Commitment (Brandom)

Normative status attributed to a speaker by uttering an assertion in [[Discursive Practice]]: the speaker is *committed to* the asserted content and to its inferential consequences. Tracked alongside [[Entitlement (Brandom)|entitlement]] in the scorekeeping picture of *Making It Explicit*.
