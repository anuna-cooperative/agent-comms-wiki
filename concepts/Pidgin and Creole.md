# Pidgin and Creole

Linguistic stages invoked as metaphors in ACL-evolution work: a *pidgin* is a simplified, unstable contact language emerging between speakers of different native tongues; a *creole* is its stabilised, grammatically-complete descendant. Agents adopting new lexicon under Rubinstein bargaining are modelled as traversing a pidgin-to-creole trajectory.
