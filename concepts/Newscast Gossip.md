## Newscast Gossip

A peer-sampling protocol in which each node periodically exchanges a fixed-size cache of fresh (peer, timestamp) entries with a random neighbour, keeping the freshest. Newscast yields a self-healing, near-uniform random overlay used as substrate for many gossip protocols.
