## Mutual Expectation

The infinite hierarchy of expectations that sustains a [[Convention (Lewis)]]: each party expects others to follow `R`, expects others to expect them to follow `R`, and so on. Equivalent to (and rediscovered as) [[Common Knowledge (Convention)|common knowledge]] in game theory and distributed systems. The operative *epistemic* condition that makes a regularity self-sustaining without explicit agreement.
