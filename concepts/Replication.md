## Replication

The [[Pi-Calculus]] operator `!P`, equivalent up to structural congruence to `P | !P` — an unbounded supply of copies of `P` running in parallel. The calculus's only source of recursive / unbounded behaviour, replacing CCS-style fixed-point definitions. Crucial for encoding services that handle arbitrarily many requests, and for the λ→π encoding (Milner 1992).
