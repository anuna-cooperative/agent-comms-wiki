# Logical Framework

A dependently-typed metalanguage (the Edinburgh **LF**) for encoding logics, their judgements, and their proofs uniformly, so that proof-checking reduces to type-checking. In Proof-Carrying Code, LF is the representation in which a producer's safety proof is shipped and a consumer mechanically validates it — the small trusted checker that makes [[Producer-Consumer Asymmetry|untrusted code]] safe to run.
