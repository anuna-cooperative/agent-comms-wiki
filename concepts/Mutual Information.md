## Mutual Information

`I(X;Y) = H(X) - H(X|Y) = H(Y) - H(Y|X)` — the reduction in uncertainty about `X` given knowledge of `Y` (and symmetrically). One of Shannon's foundational quantities ([[A Mathematical Theory of Communication]]); the operational basis of [[Channel Capacity]] (`C = max_{p(x)} I(X;Y)`). Used in MAS as a measure of [[Causal Influence of Communication|causal communication influence]] in emergent-language metrics ([[On the Pitfalls of Measuring Emergent Communication]]).
