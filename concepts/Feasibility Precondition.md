# Feasibility Precondition

In the FIPA-ACL / Cohen–Levesque semantics of communicative acts, the condition that must hold for an agent to sincerely perform a speech act — paired with the *rational effect*, the result the agent intends to bring about. For an `inform`, the feasibility precondition requires (roughly) that the sender believes the proposition and believes the receiver does not already hold a view on it. The FP/RE pair is the core of [[Mentalistic Semantics]].
