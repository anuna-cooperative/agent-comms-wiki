# Normative Position

An agent's total bundle of jural relations at a point in a normative system — what it is obliged, permitted, forbidden, empowered, or liable to do with respect to others. Made precise through [[Hohfeldian Analysis]] (claim/duty, privilege/no-right, power/liability, immunity/disability) and built up from [[Constitutive Rules]] and [[Empowerment]]; the unit normative MAS reason over when deciding whether an action is institutionally in order.
