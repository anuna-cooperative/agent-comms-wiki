## Second-Price Auction

Synonym for [[Vickrey Auction]]. The pricing rule — winner pays the second-highest bid — is what makes truth-telling a dominant strategy: the price the winner pays is the externality they impose on losing bidders, aligning private incentive with social efficiency.
