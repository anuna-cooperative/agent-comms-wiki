## Self-Adaptation

A system's capability to modify its own behaviour or structure at runtime in response to internal or environmental change, without external intervention. It is typically implemented as a [[MAPE-K]] loop (Monitor-Analyse-Plan-Execute over a Knowledge base), the control abstraction introduced by [[Autonomic Computing]].
