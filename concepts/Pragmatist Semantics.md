# Pragmatist Semantics

Family of approaches that ground meaning in *use* — communicative practices, normative attitudes, [[Commitment (Brandom)|commitments]] and [[Entitlement (Brandom)|entitlements]] — rather than in mind-independent reference. Brandom's *Making It Explicit* is the canonical contemporary statement.
