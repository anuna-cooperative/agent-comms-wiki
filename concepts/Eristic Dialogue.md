## Eristic Dialogue

A [[Dialogue Typology|Walton-Krabbe dialogue type]] whose shared goal is *to vent emotion or display superiority* — the dialogue type in which "winning" is the participant goal and there is no shared epistemic or practical aim. Pre-modern rhetoric and many social-media exchanges fit this type. In MAS the relevant question is not how to *engage* in eristic dialogues but how to *detect* and exit them — a multi-agent system inadvertently conducting eristic dialogues at scale is a [[Why Do Multi-Agent LLM Systems Fail|MAST]]-style failure.
