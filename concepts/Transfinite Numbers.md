# Transfinite Numbers

Cantor's extension of counting and ordering beyond the finite: the transfinite [[Ordinals]] (order types of well-orderings: ω, ω+1, …) and transfinite cardinals (sizes: ℵ₀, ℵ₁, …). Introduced to make precise the idea that infinite collections can be compared and arithmetic done on them. Given canonical set-theoretic form by the [[Ordinals|von Neumann ordinals]].
