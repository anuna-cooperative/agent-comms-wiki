# Distributed Knowledge

A group-level epistemic operator (`D_G φ`): the knowledge that *would* result if the members of group `G` pooled everything they individually know. It is the strongest of the standard group notions — implied by any single member's knowledge and itself implying that the group could, by communicating, come to know φ — sitting above "everyone knows" and [[Common Knowledge]] in strength only in the sense of combining information (common knowledge is harder to *attain*). Formally, `D_G φ` holds at a world when φ is true in every world consistent with the *intersection* of the members' [[Kripke Structure|accessibility]] relations.
