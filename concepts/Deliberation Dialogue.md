## Deliberation Dialogue

A [[Dialogue Typology|Walton-Krabbe dialogue type]] whose shared goal is *to decide on a course of action* in the face of an open practical question. Unlike [[Persuasion Dialogue|persuasion]] (about what is true), deliberation is about what to do. McBurney, Hitchcock & Parsons (2007) gave a formal protocol for deliberation dialogues; in MAS, deliberation is the natural target of multi-agent planning, [[Joint Intentions]], and group decision-making.
