# Data Processing

In the Kermarrec–van Steen three-parameter gossip framework, the component of a gossip protocol that decides *what to do* with state received from a peer (overwrite, merge, aggregate, filter). Together with peer selection and data exchange, it parameterises the space of gossip protocols.
