# Negotiated Protocols

In Agora (Marro et al.), communication protocols that pairs of LLM agents agree upon on demand — typically bootstrapped via natural-language meta-negotiation and crystallised into a hash-identified Protocol Document. Negotiation decouples design-time standardisation from runtime interoperability.
