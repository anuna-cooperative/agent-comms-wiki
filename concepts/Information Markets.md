# Information Markets

Markets in which the good traded is information. They are structurally distinct from goods markets because of the [[Arrow Information Paradox|Arrow / buyer's-inspection paradox]]: buyers need access to evaluate, sellers need to restrict access to prevent theft. AI agents with *evaluate + forget* capability and / or [[Trusted Execution Environment|TEE]]-mediated disclosure can serve as trusted intermediaries that resolve the paradox.
