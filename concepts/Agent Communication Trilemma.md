## Agent Communication Trilemma

The claim that agent communication design faces three competing demands — expressiveness, efficiency, and interoperability — and that any protocol can satisfy at most two strongly. LLM-based agent frameworks often surface this as a tension between natural-language richness, token cost, and machine-checkable structure.
