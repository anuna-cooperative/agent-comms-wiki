# Confirmation Holism

Quine's *Two Dogmas* (1951) thesis: empirical confirmation always falls on a body of theory, never on isolated sentences. Together with the [[Quine-Duhem Thesis]] and the [[Web of Belief]] image, motivates [[Semantic Holism]].
