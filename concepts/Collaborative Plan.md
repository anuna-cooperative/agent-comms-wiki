## Collaborative Plan

Synonym for [[SharedPlans|SharedPlan]] in the Grosz-Kraus framework: a plan structure for joint action that is not reducible to a union of individual plans, carrying mutual beliefs about who does what, recipes for sub-actions, and the agents' intentions toward each part. Operationalises Bratman's [[Shared Cooperative Activity]] in a planning-theoretic setting suitable for AI implementation.
