# Sapience vs Sentience

Brandom / Pittsburgh-School distinction: *sentience* is the capacity for awareness (shared with non-linguistic animals), *sapience* is the capacity to play the game of giving and asking for reasons in the [[Space of Reasons]]. Only sapient beings have conceptual content.
