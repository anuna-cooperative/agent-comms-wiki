# Mental Attitudes

The propositional attitudes (belief, desire, intention, knowledge) that agents hold toward content in the mentalistic tradition of ACL semantics. In Singh's common ontology of ACLs they are formalised as the private substrate against which "sincerity"-style preconditions are unverifiable without introspection.
