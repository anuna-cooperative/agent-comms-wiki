## Kademlia

Maymounkov & Mazières's (2002) [[Distributed Hash Table]] organised around the [[XOR Metric]] on identifier space, with [[k-Buckets|k-buckets]] biased toward long-lived nodes, iterative parallel lookups, and opportunistic routing-table maintenance. The DHT underlying BitTorrent Mainline, [[IPFS Content-Addressed Versioned P2P File System|IPFS]] / libp2p, Ethereum Discv4/Discv5, Tor hidden services, I2P, and most production P2P systems.
