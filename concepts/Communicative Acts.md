# Communicative Acts

FIPA's term for the primitive message types (INFORM, REQUEST, PROPOSE, CFP, AGREE, REFUSE, ...) whose semantics is defined in SL modal logic over the sender's beliefs, intentions and feasibility preconditions. Effectively the FIPA-ACL rebranding of KQML's performatives.
