# Word Embeddings

Dense, low-dimensional real-valued vectors that represent words such that distributional similarity becomes geometric proximity — the modern realisation of [[Distributional Semantics]]. Predictive learning methods (word2vec's skip-gram / CBOW, GloVe) induce embeddings in which related words cluster and some relations appear as consistent vector offsets (the *king − man + woman ≈ queen* analogy). Static word embeddings were the precursor to the contextual representations learned inside transformer [[LLM Agents|language models]].
