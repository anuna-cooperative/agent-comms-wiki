## Behaviour-Based Robotics

The research programme inaugurated by Brooks's [[A Robust Layered Control System|subsumption architecture]] (1986) and *Intelligence Without Representation* (1991): build robotic intelligence by composing concurrent behavioural competencies (each a complete sense-act loop) rather than by decomposing into functional modules (perception / world-model / planning / control). Rejects central symbolic representation in favour of letting "the world be its own best model." Influenced multi-agent systems through the [[Reactive vs Deliberative Agents]] dichotomy.
