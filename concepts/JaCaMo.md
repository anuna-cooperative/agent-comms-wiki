# JaCaMo

An integrated multi-agent programming platform combining Jason (AgentSpeak BDI agents), CArtAgO (environments as artifacts), and Moise (organisational structures), instantiating the Agents & Artifacts metamodel. Cited as an interaction-oriented framework for open MAS.
