# Content Languages

Languages (KIF, SL, Prolog, RDF) used to express the *content* of an ACL message independently of the outer performative/message wrapper. The separation of wrapper from content language is an explicit FIPA/KQML design choice enabling heterogeneity and ontology reuse.
