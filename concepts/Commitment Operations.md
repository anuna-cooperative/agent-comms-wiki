# Commitment Operations

The lifecycle operations on a [[Social Commitment]] in Singh's ontology: **create**, **discharge** (satisfy), **cancel** (debtor withdraws), **release** (creditor frees the debtor), **delegate** (transfer the debtor role), and **assign** (transfer the creditor role). Because a commitment is a manipulable first-class object rather than a mental state, these public operations give agent interaction a verifiable, protocol-level semantics.
