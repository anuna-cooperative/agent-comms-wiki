## Boundary

In the [[Ambient Calculus]] (and engineering systems generally) — the syntactic / runtime delimiter of an administrative domain. An ambient `n[P]` is a process `P` enclosed in a named boundary `n`; mobility is boundary-crossing, governed by [[Capabilities (Ambient)]]. The notion generalises across firewalls (network boundary), processes (OS boundary), containers, sandboxes, and capability-secured object graphs ([[Capability Security]]). The choice of boundary placement is a primary architectural decision in any distributed-system design.
