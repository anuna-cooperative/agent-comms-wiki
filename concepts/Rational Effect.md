## Rational Effect

In FIPA-ACL, the effect a sender of a communicative act intends to bring about in the recipient (e.g. that the hearer come to believe a proposition). Because it concerns private mental state it is unverifiable, and is the principal target of critiques that call for grounded or social semantics.
