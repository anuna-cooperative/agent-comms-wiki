## Higher-Order Pi-Calculus

A variant of the [[Pi-Calculus]] in which *processes themselves* (not only names) may be communicated. Sangiorgi (1992) proved that higher-order π is reducible to first-order (name-passing) π by a CPS-style encoding — passing a process is operationally equivalent to passing a *trigger name* together with replicating the process to receive on that name. The reduction shows that name-passing alone is expressively complete; higher-order forms are convenient but not fundamental.
