# Choreography Amendment

The (mechanisable) transformation that takes a [[Choreographic Programming|choreography]] lacking [[Knowledge of Choice|knowledge of choice]] and inserts the missing [[Selection (Choreography)|selections]] so that every participant whose behaviour depends on a choice is told which branch was taken — restoring realisability as deadlock-free local programs while preserving observable behaviour. Also called *repair*; automated procedures were given by Lanese, Guidi, Montesi & Zavattaro (2013), Basu & Bultan (2016), and Cruz-Filipe & Montesi (2020, 2023).
