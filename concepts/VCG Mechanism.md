## VCG Mechanism

The **Vickrey-Clarke-Groves** mechanism: a generalisation of the [[Vickrey Auction|Vickrey second-price auction]] to multi-item, combinatorial, and public-goods allocation problems (Vickrey 1961, Clarke 1971, Groves 1973). Each participant pays the *externality* they impose on the rest — the difference between the social welfare of the optimal allocation without their participation and the social welfare of the rest in the actual allocation. Truth-telling is a dominant strategy. Underpins almost all sponsored-search auctions (Google AdWords, Facebook Ads), spectrum auctions, and academic combinatorial-auction designs.
