# Transformational Grammar

Chomsky's proposal in *Three Models* that natural-language syntax is best described by a small kernel of simple sentence patterns plus a set of *transformations* (passive, question, negation) that derive surface sentences — yielding grammars more compact and explanatory than unaugmented phrase-structure rules.
