## Meshing Subplans

Bratman's (1992) construction in [[Shared Cooperative Activity]]: in genuine cooperative joint action, each participant forms a subplan for their own contribution, and the subplans must *mesh* — be positively coordinated rather than merely consistent. Stronger than non-interference: the subplans must positively support each other and not inadvertently undermine each other's success conditions. Computational realisation in [[SharedPlans]] (Grosz & Kraus 1996).
