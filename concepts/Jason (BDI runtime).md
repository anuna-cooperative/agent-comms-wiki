## Jason (BDI runtime)

Bordini, Hübner & Wooldridge's (2007) Java-based interpreter for an extended [[AgentSpeak]] language — the most-deployed [[BDI]] agent platform in academic MAS research. Adds internal actions, customisable selection functions, plan annotation, and integration with [[Agents and Artifacts|JaCaMo]] ([[CArtAgO]] artefacts + MOISE organisations). Used as the standard testbed in many MAS research papers and as the implementation substrate of several agent-coordination projects.
