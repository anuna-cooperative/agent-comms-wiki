## OTP

Erlang's Open Telecom Platform: a library of behaviours (gen_server, gen_statem, supervisor, application) and design principles that codify fault-tolerant, concurrent systems construction. OTP bakes the "let it crash" philosophy and supervision trees into reusable templates.
