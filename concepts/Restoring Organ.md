# Restoring Organ

In von Neumann's [[Multiplexing (von Neumann)|multiplexing]] construction, the nonlinear stage placed after each bundle-gate that pushes the bundle's stimulation level back toward 0 or 1. Because ordinary gates degrade the separation between "almost all lines on" and "almost all off", an explicit restoring map (a sigmoid-like majority operation) is needed to prevent error from drifting toward the ambiguous middle and accumulating across stages.
