## Information-Seeking Dialogue

A [[Dialogue Typology|Walton-Krabbe dialogue type]] whose shared goal is the *transfer of information from an informed party to an uninformed party*. Asymmetric in starting epistemic state — one participant has knowledge the other lacks. The bulk of LLM-chat use cases instantiate this type; many of the failure modes of LLM agents arise when an information-seeking dialogue *shifts* (without the user's awareness) into a persuasion or eristic dialogue.
