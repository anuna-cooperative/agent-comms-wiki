# An Axiomatic Basis for Computer Programming

Hoare 1969: introduces [[Hoare Triple|Hoare triples]] {P} S {Q} and the assignment, sequence, conditional, and while axioms that allow programs to be reasoned about as logical sentences. Founding paper of axiomatic semantics.
