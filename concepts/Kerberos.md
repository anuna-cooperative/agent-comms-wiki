## Kerberos

The MIT Project Athena authentication system (1988), the most widely-deployed practical descendant of the [[Needham-Schroeder Protocol|Needham-Schroeder symmetric-key protocol]]. Uses a *Key Distribution Center* (KDC) split into an Authentication Server and a Ticket Granting Server; principals obtain time-limited *tickets* encrypted under shared keys. The basis of Active Directory authentication and most enterprise SSO.
