# McCulloch-Pitts Neuron

The 1943 threshold-logic unit: a binary element that "fires" (outputs 1) when the weighted sum of its inputs meets a threshold, otherwise outputs 0. Networks of such units can compute any Boolean function, which is why von Neumann adopts it as the *basic organ* whose unreliable behaviour his reliability construction must tolerate. Also the historical seed of artificial neural networks.
