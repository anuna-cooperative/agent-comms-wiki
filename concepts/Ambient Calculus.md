## Ambient Calculus

The process calculus of Cardelli & Gordon (1998) in which the primary mobile entity is a named bounded region (an *ambient*) rather than a channel name as in the [[Pi-Calculus]]. Movement is expressed by the three capabilities `in n`, `out n`, `open n`. Captures administrative-domain structure (firewalls, address spaces, hosts) directly in the syntax. See [[Mobile Ambients]].
