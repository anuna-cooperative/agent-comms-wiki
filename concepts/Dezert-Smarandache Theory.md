# Dezert-Smarandache Theory

A generalisation of Dempster-Shafer evidence combination (DSmT) that handles paradoxical, conflicting, and overlapping hypotheses by operating on the hyper-power-set of a frame of discernment. Used to fuse direct experience with neighbour opinions in self-organising agent networks where trust signals may contradict.
