# Disclosure Decisions

An agent's choice of whether, and what, personal or sensitive information to reveal in a particular interaction. The behavioural output of [[Privacy Reasoning]]: under [[Contextual Integrity]] a disclosure is appropriate when it conforms to the information-flow norms of the context, and the hard cases studied are precisely those where the context is ambiguous and the norm must be inferred rather than looked up.
