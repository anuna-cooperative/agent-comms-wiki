## SharedPlans

Grosz & Kraus's (1996) computational framework for collaborative joint action: a SharedPlan is a group-level structure carrying [[Mutual Belief|mutual beliefs]] about who is doing what, why, and how, with two intention modalities (`Int.To` for own actions, `Int.Th` for actions of others or the group). Most real collaboration involves *partial* SharedPlans, refined incrementally through meta-intentions to complete the plan, help others, and communicate. The canonical computational realisation of Bratman's [[Shared Cooperative Activity]].
