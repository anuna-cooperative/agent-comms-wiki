## BDI Logic

A family of multimodal logics (Rao & Georgeff, Cohen & Levesque) that formalise the attitudes of Belief, Desire and Intention, giving axioms for their interaction over branching time. BDI logic underpins mentalistic semantics for agent communication languages.
