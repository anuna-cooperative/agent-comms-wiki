# Sensitivity Sharing

The core mechanism of the Ripple Effect Protocol: agents exchange not decisions but *counterfactual sensitivities* — how much their chosen action would change in response to peers's hypothetical actions — allowing populations of LLM agents to anticipate cascades without full state sharing.
