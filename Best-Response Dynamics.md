# Best-Response Dynamics

A learning/adjustment process in games where each player iteratively updates their strategy to the action that is optimal given others' current strategies. Convergence to Nash equilibria is guaranteed in potential games but the equilibria reached can be catastrophically poor when communication is partial, as shown by the resilience analysis of Gopalakrishnan et al.
