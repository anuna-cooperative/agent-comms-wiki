# Conversations

Concrete instances of a conversation/interaction protocol shared between specific agents, typically tracked by a conversation-id and represented as a Coloured Petri Net or Dooley Graph marking in engines like ACRE. Conversations make protocol state first-class so plans can reason about whether a dialogue is in progress, completed, or stuck.
