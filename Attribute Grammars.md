# Attribute Grammars

A formalism extending context-free grammars with semantic *attributes* attached to nonterminals and *equations* defining them from children/parent attributes, used to specify compositional static semantics. They underpin extensible-language frameworks (Silver, Extensibella) where new syntax contributes its own attribute equations modularly.
