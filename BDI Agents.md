# BDI Agents

Agents whose architecture is structured around explicit Beliefs (information about the world), Desires (goals), and Intentions (committed plans). The BDI model provides a practical reasoning cycle — deliberation over desires yields intentions, means-ends reasoning selects plans — and is the basis of AgentSpeak, Jason, JACK and similar platforms.
