# Knowledge Representation Language

An agent's private, internal formalism for representing beliefs, goals, and world models — typically a frame-based, logic-based, or probabilistic language distinct from the shared ACL used on the wire. In ACL-evolution models each agent maintains a transducer between its KRL and the evolving public language.
