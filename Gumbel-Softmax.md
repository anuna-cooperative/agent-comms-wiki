# Gumbel-Softmax

A continuous, differentiable relaxation of categorical sampling (Jang et al., Maddison et al.) using Gumbel noise and a temperature-annealed softmax. Enables end-to-end gradient training through discrete-symbol communication channels in emergent-language experiments.
